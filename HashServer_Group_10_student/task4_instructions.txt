Task 4 (Length-Extension Vulnerability Demonstration) — Group 10

You are given:
  - message.txt   : the original message (m)
  - tag.hex       : the original tag = SHA1(key || m), where key is secret
  - keylen_hint.txt : a hint for the secret key length (range)

Goal:
  Produce a forged message m' and forged tag t' such that:
    t' = SHA1(key || m')
  where:
    m' = m || padding || "&role=admin"

Required deliverables for Task 4:
  1) forged_message.txt  (your m')
  2) forged_tag.hex      (your t')

Notes:
  - The construction SHA1(key||message) is INSECURE for Merkle–Damgård hashes because
    an attacker can extend the message using length-extension.
  - You do NOT know the key. You may need to guess the key length within the provided range.
  - You do not need to recover the key.

Submission check:
  Your instructor will verify your forged pair (m', t') using the hidden key assigned to your group.
